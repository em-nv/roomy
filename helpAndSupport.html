FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

  1. How do I search for apartments on Roomy?

    2. -To search for apartments, simply enter your desired location, preferred features, and budget into our search bar on the homepage. Browse through the listings and click on a property to view more details.

  2. How can I reserve an apartment?

    3. -Once you've found an apartment you're interested in, use our reservation feature to schedule a visit or communicate directly with the landlord or property manager.

  3. What should I do if I encounter an issue during the reservation process?

    4. -If you face any challenges or encounter technical issues while reserving an apartment, please reach out to our support team via support@roomy.com.

  4. Are utilities included in the rent?

    5. -Utility inclusions vary by property. You can find information about utilities included or excluded in each listing's details.

  5. How can I edit or cancel a reservation?

    6. -To edit or cancel a reservation, please log in to your Roomy account, navigate to your reservations, and follow the provided instructions.
